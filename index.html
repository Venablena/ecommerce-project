Champion This Month

Rampion

Root white, spindle-shaped, and nearly 1/2 inch in diameter for 2 inches or more of its length; a flesh white, very firm, but crisp; leaves sessile, rather numerous, of a long oval-spathulate shape.

Daily Classic

Drumhead Cabbage

A very distinct variety, with an extremely short stem. Head very hard and compact, rather broader than long, and somewhat bulging in the upper part.

New and Exotic

Monstrous Capsicum

The seed-vessels are of an irregular ovoid or conical shape, swollen in the part next to the stalk and narrowed at the other end, usually more abruptly on one side than the other.
